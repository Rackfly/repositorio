Welcome to the GameCube Vault. This Vault contains every known GameCube game in the world, scanned nightly by Redump. To play them you'll need an emulator from the Emulation Lair. This Vault contains 1966 of 1969 known GameCube discs, catalogued by Redump on April 12, 2023. If you have a clean Redump copy of a disc that's missing, please upload it! All downloads are in .7z format and can be opened with the free tool 7-Zip. All games are in .ciso format. Disc and cover scans are provided by GameTDB.
